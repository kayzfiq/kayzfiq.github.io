My path into cybersecurity started with networking. After earning my CCNA certification, I gained a solid understanding of how data flows, how networks are structured, and‚Äîcrucially‚Äîwhere vulnerabilities exist. But I realized that to truly protect systems, I needed to understand them at a deeper level.
